KNOW US

CHARUSAT Hospital – Making Communities Healthier

To serve the society and community in a better way, CHARUSAT Healthcare and Research Foundation (CHRF) a not-for-profit organization has established a world-class multispecialty CHARUSAT hospital, holding motto of ‘Care with Compassion’.

CHARUSAT hospital has been conducting various activities in the areas of Child Care, Woman Care, Healthcare related to lifestyle diseases and more. It also undertakes awareness programs and conducting camps in nearby villages for providing effective & timely healthcare facilities to the community.

CHARUSAT Hospital is also providing clinical training and quality healthcare education to the students in the area of Physiotherapy, Nursing, Pharmacy, and Paramedical sciences. It is functional since 2015, with 100 bed capacity with a team of doctors and supporting staff. Presently, it is treatment with 100 bed capacity with a vision to expand upto 450 beds.

CHARUSAT hospital is designed with emphasis on excellence in clinical services, diagnostic facilities and research. It has most modern operation theatres, ICU, NICU, Out Patient Department (OPD), 24 hours emergency services, Blood bank and well-equipped laboratory facilities.


QUALITY ACCREDITATION

CHARUSAT Hospital has received accreditation from the National Accreditation Board for Hospital and Healthcare providers (NABH). It is the first standalone charitable multispecialty hospital of Charotar region to be awarded accreditation from the NABH.


OBJECTIVES OF CHRF

  • To make available affordable Preventive and Curative Healthcare service to all classes of Community without discrimination of caste, creed, religion race or region through establishment of Hospitals, Dispensaries, Health centers, Diagnostic labs, etc.
  • To participate in the initiatives of the Government and other relevant synergetic organizations regarding healthcare, population welfare, vaccination, emergency medical services, healthcare awareness.
  • To undertake surveys, awareness programs, and outreach programs for healthcare amelioration of community.
  • To make use of prevalent & innovative technologies in delivering healthcare services.
  • To undertake research & development in the areas of healthcare & allied services.
  • To organize seminars, lectures, training programs, need based certification programs for healthcare field and other such programs for strengthening healthcare systems of society.
  • To disseminate knowledge and spread awareness regarding preventive care, hygiene, dietary habits and nutrition intake and other such are as related to healthcare.
